What exactly is "nesting"?

Nesting is the placement of one or more objects within another object. For example, when referring to a computer, nesting may refer to inserting a graphic image into a word processor. In computer terms this is called the irregular bin-packing problem.

In an object maker’s technical term, you may have some parts to cut out of a piece of metal, plastic, wood, etc., and you'd want to arrange the parts to use as little material as possible. This is a common problem if you use a laser cutter, plasma cutter, or CNC machine.

For the textile cutters for clothes, this software applies a superior mathematical algorithm and artificial intelligence to offer the best nesting solution.

Can I change the straight grains for certain pattern parts?

Yes, starting from the Standard plan, you can choose certain pattern parts and change their straight grains. If you’re working with some finishing details like waist bands, it can help you optimize your textile consumption while maintaining the quality of the clothes. Certain fabrics can give you more diverse options than others, too. Please contact the team to learn more about the zero waste pattern production guide.

Does it use inches? mm?

DXFnest’s basic units are cm, mm, and inch. You can change them by choosing the right option.

My DXF text/image doesn't show up?

The nesting algorithm only works for closed shapes. All the pattern elements that don't represent closed shapes are automatically removed from the DXFnest software. Convert text and any other elements to outlines first, if they exist outside the patterns. Please ensure that outlines do not intersect or overlap each other. Outlines that are inside other outlines are considered annotations.

It doesn't ever stop?

The software will continuously look for better solutions until you press the stop button. You can stop at any time and download the DXF file.

Some parts seem to slightly overlap?

Curved shapes are approximated with line segments. For a more accurate nest with curved parts, decrease the curve tolerance parameter in the configuration.
